FINAL PROJECT PITCHING AT 1:30

Each group will have roughly 15 minutes to present their game idea. Remember to include:

  1. A working title
  2. Clear description of the game's concept, theme, and goals.
  3. Description of your game's core mechanics through the use of compelling verbs.
  4. Two precedents for your core mechanics which are successful & relevant to your game.
  5. Two examples of concept art which are representative of your game's visuals.
  6. Two reference/inspirational images which are informing your concept art or overall visual aesthetic.
  7. Two precedents for your game's UI using successful examples from similar games.
  8. Clarification of each team member's expected roles.
