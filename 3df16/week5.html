MIDTERM PROJECT: VERSUS (DUE 10/25)

In groups of 3, you will complete a single local multiplayer level, which pits one player against another.

  • Game can use shooting mechanics... or not!
  • Focus on designing a fun and engaging level which supports your mechanics
  • Cohesive and memorable theme which is developed through sounds, visuals, and level design
  • Game must have a clear win condition
  • Splitscreen or shared camera
  • No humanoid IK animation on players. Work around the problem!
  • All members must contribute to building the systems or designing the level at some point during the development process

MIDTERM PROJECT: BRAINSTORMING, RESEARCH, & CONCEPTUALIZING

At 1:30 next week, each group will have 10 minutes to present their game idea. This should include:

  • Clear description of the game's concept and theme.
  • Description of the game's systems, including goals, conflict, and core mechanics.
  • Demonstration of two core mechanics which you've prototyped
  • Preliminary sketches and first pass at a greyboxed level
  • Sketches and concept art, with emphasis on how they support the concept
  • Clarification of each team member's expected roles
